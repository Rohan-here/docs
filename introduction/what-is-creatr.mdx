Welcome to the official Creatr documentation — your definitive resource for building production-ready web applications without getting bogged down in technical details. With Creatr, you can transform your creative ideas into fully functional web apps, all while leaving the complexities of coding behind.

## What is Creatr?

Creatr is an innovative platform that empowers everyone — from aspiring entrepreneurs to seasoned professionals — to **create products** with ease. Our solution removes the technical barriers of traditional web development, so you can focus solely on your vision. 

- **Building Modular Components:** Create unique UI elements on the fly — from popups and buttons to input fields and carousels.
- **Cloning & Customizing Websites:** Instantly replicate any website and transform it into a fully editable canvas.
- **Integrating Your Stack:** Enhance your application with powerful integrations like **Supabase** for backend support, **OpenAI** for AI-driven features, and **Resend** for email services — with many more on the horizon.
- **Production-Ready Performance:** Powered by a Next.js-based stack, your web apps are built to be fast, secure, and scalable from day one.
