Welcome to the official Creatr documentation — your gateway to an AI-powered development experience that redefines how you build, customize, and deploy web applications. Creatr leverages advanced AI and LLMs to help designers, developers, and entrepreneurs craft beautiful components, clone entire websites, and integrate seamlessly with their favorite tools.

## What is Creatr?

Creatr is a platform designed to transform your creative ideas into reality by:
- **Building Modular Components:** Create unique UI elements on the fly — from popups and buttons to input fields and carousels.
- **Cloning & Customizing Websites:** Instantly replicate any website and transform it into a fully editable canvas.
- **Integrating Your Stack:** Connect with popular services like Stripe, Clerk, and Supabase for payments, storage, authentication, and more.
- **Accelerating Development:** Generate complete web apps in minutes with near-perfect accuracy, streamlining your workflow.
